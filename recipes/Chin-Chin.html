Chin-Chin

Description

Chin-chin is a popular and crunchy Nigerian snack that is loved by people of all ages. It is a delicious deep-fried pastry made from a simple dough consisting of flour, sugar, butter, and a touch of nutmeg for flavor. The dough is rolled out, cut into small squares or other shapes, and then fried until golden and crispy. Chin-chin is enjoyed as a delightful treat during festive occasions, as a tasty accompaniment to beverages, or simply as a flavorful snack to satisfy cravings. Its addictive crunch and subtly sweet taste make it a beloved snack in Nigeria and among the Nigerian diaspora worldwide.

Prepare the Dough: ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 2 tablespoons butter, softened
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Steps.

  1. a. In a large bowl, mix the flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, and ground nutmeg. b. Add the softened butter and use your fingers to rub it into the dry ingredients until the mixture resembles breadcrumbs. c. In a separate small bowl, beat the egg, then add it to the flour mixture. d. Gradually add the milk and mix until a stiff dough forms. Knead the dough for a few minutes until smooth.
  2. Roll and Cut the Chin Chin Instructions: a. On a floured surface, roll out the dough to a thickness of about 1/4 inch. b. Use a knife or a pizza cutter to cut the dough into small squares or desired shapes..
  3. Fry the Chin Chin Instructions: a. In a deep saucepan or frying pan, heat vegetable oil to about 350°F (175°C). b. Fry the Chin Chin in batches until golden brown and crispy, stirring occasionally to ensure even frying. c. Remove the fried Chin Chin with a slotted spoon and place them on paper towels to drain excess oil..
  4. Cool and Serve Instructions: a. Allow the fried Chin Chin to cool completely. b. Once cooled, transfer them to an airtight container for storage or serve them as a delightful and crunchy snack.!
